Good Manufacturing Practice (GMP) SOP

Reactor Temperature Procedure

1. Reactor temperature must remain between 35°C and 45°C.

2. If temperature exceeds 50°C:

- Stop the production batch.

- Notify the quality assurance team.

- Inspect cooling systems.

- Record the event.

- Initiate root cause analysis.

3. Pressure must remain between 95 and 110 kPa.

4. Yield below 80% is considered critical.

5. Critical events must be escalated immediately.
